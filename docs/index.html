WHO WE ARE

Our Mission

At Home For All, we believe that every child deserves safety, love, and a place to call home.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between vulnerable children and verified orphanages that can provide proper care and rehabilitation.

Through our secure platform, citizens can report orphaned children, ensuring quick and verified responses.

WHAT WE DO

How We Help Orphans

From initial rescue to long-term rehabilitation, we provide comprehensive support to ensure every child finds their path to a brighter future.

Reporting & Rescue Support

Our secure platform allows verified citizens to report orphaned children. Each report triggers immediate rescue coordination.

Health & Safety Assistance

Every rescued child receives medical attention, nutrition, hygiene care, and psychological support.

Orphanage Connectivity

We connect rescued children with verified orphanages through safe and trusted handover processes.

Long-term Rehabilitation

We support education, skill development, and emotional well-being for a better future.
